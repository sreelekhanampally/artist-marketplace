Hi, I'm Siri 🌸

Siri is an award-winning art director & illustrator in New York City. Her work appears in major campaigns for: Coca-Cola, Uniqlo, Google, The Democrats, VICE, and more. Loe's extensive background in design and branding guide her creative process. With experience as both vendor and client, Loe understands the collaboration process inside and out - from brainstorming 360° campaigns to delivering optimized files for digital, print, and motion. Loe also enjoys giving talks and workshops at corporate events and creative festivals. When she’s not drawing, Loe is trying new Cantonese recipes or cuddling her two big dogs.
